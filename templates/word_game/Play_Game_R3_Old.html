Rules for Round 3


Overview: As before, spell the highest-valued word that you can. But this time the tiles come in different colors, and you can’t have two tiles of the same color in a row. (If you don’t see colors well, you can use the tile shapes.)

  • In Stage 3, there are 16 rounds of 4 minutes and 45 seconds each.
  • However, in Stage 3 you can only spell words in which no two consecutive tiles have the same color.
  • If you don't see color well, you can use the shape, because the shape and color of a tile are always consistent. Green tiles are hexagons, red tiles are circles, and blue tiles are squares.
